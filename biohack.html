📘 How to Use

  1. Paste a full genome sequence into the Source DNA area.
  2. Load a preset – click "Horse" or "Car" on the right.
  3. Edit gene pool: Click 🧪/🧬 to toggle mode, or click A,C,G,T buttons to manage bases.
  4. Save custom preset – click "Save current preset".
  5. Copy Flask 1 and Flask 2, then paste into game flasks.
  6. Drain vat, add flasks, press red button.
